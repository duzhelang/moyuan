技术架构变更：sequenceDiagram
    participant FE as 前端
    participant FC as FileController
    participant FSS as FileStorageService
    participant WU as WatermarkUtil
    participant DB as MySQL
    participant FS as 文件系统

    FE->>FC: POST /api/files/upload
    FC->>FSS: upload(file, fileType, relatedId, relatedType)
    FSS->>FS: calculateMd5(file)
    FSS->>DB: 查询MD5是否存在
    alt MD5不存在
        FSS->>FS: transferTo(destFile)
        alt ai_generated类型
            FSS->>WU: addAiWatermark(image)
            WU-->>FSS: watermarkedImage
            FSS->>FS: 覆盖写入水印图片
        end
        FSS->>DB: insert(file_metadata)
    end
    FSS-->>FC: FileMetadata
    FC-->>FE: R<Map>

业务流程变更：
flowchart TD
    A[前端上传请求] --> B{是否携带fileType?}
    B -->|是| C[FileStorageService.upload]
    B -->|否| D[FileController旧逻辑]
    C --> E[构建子目录路径]
    E --> F[计算MD5]
    F --> G{MD5已存在?}
    G -->|是| H[复用旧记录]
    G -->|否| I[写入磁盘]
    I --> J{是否AI生成图片?}
    J -->|是| K[添加水印]
    J -->|否| L[读取宽高]
    K --> L
    L --> M[保存元数据到DB]
    M --> N[返回文件信息]
